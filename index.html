WELCOME TO AAKRUTHI 2022
The Technical Symposium of the ECE Department, OUCE is the brain child of an erudite group of students, who under the guidance of the eminent faculty, have initiated this event with a view to mobilize the skills of students towards devising optimal solutions through practical problems while catering to the best needs of the industry. The Department of ECE has been consistent in bolstering this prestigious event, playing a pivotal role in making it one of the best platforms for students to hone their technical skills and set a path for their bright careers. Owing to the persistent effort on behalf of students, the event has received an overwhelming response from different parts of the state, Last year around 800 students have participated.
ABOUT ECE DEPARTMENT
The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) was established in the year 1959 with only two faculty members. It bears the indelible stamp of its founder Head of the Department, late Prof. K. Krishnan Nair. He exhorted ECE Department to become a name for Excellence, Creativity and Enterprise. In the span of five decades, the Department has grown to a complement of 16 members of teaching staff and about 500 students at UG and PG, with the staff specialised in various fields of Digital Systems, Control Systems, Signal Processing, Microwaves, Microprocessor Applications, Computer System, Communication Engineering, Embedded Systems, VLSI Design etc., and most of them having Doctorates. The broad spectrum of subjects in the area of ECE is fully represented. Areas of research include VLSI, Embedded Systems, Digital signal Processing and Microwave Engineering.

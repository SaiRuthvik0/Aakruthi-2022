Ethical hacking and Cybersecurity Workshop

AAKRUTHI 2022 presents a two day hands on live workshop on Ethical Hacking and Cybersecurity with internship opportunity.
Workshop Objectives:
At the end of this Workshop, participants will be able to:
1. Understand, perform and secure themselves from various kinds of Hacking attacks.
2. Understand Email attacks and will be able to protect their email accounts as well as social networking accounts from these attacks.
3. Understand Web Application Attacks and will be able to patch them to produce a secure web application.
